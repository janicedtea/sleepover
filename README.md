Welcome to Rem Run. A platformer game made in Godot by Raaina, Janice, and Monika for HackClub’s Sleepover!

Follow Marshmallow, a very squishy rabbit, as they travel through their dream for the night. Based off of the REM cycle, each stage of the Marshmallow’s dream progressively becomes more surreal as you solve riddles, go through parkour, and interact with enemies. Ensure that Marshmallow makes it through the night with a restful sleep to wake up the next morning!

Some basic controls and instructions!
Press E to interact with NPCs. To progress through dialogue, press enter. Please go through all dialogue before moving on. Use arrow keys to move, and space to jump.
